About

The basiCha website is created to support students in mastering the Cha-Cha dance through engaging, interactive, and multimedia-based e-learning materials. It aims to enhance dance education by offering step-by-step video tutorials, animations, and self-paced activities that cater to different learning styles.

This platform serves as a valuable tool for students, teachers and dance instructors for making Physical Education more accessible, fun, and effective. By combining creativity with technology, basiCha encourages learners to build their confidence, rhythm, and dance skills anytime and anywhere, promoting mastery learning in a modern and student-friendly way.
